## Introduction

365Weapons ("we," "our," or "us") operates the 365Weapons Admin mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you use the App, we may collect the following personal information:

| Data Type | Purpose | Linked to Identity |
|-----------|---------|-------------------|
| **Email Address** | Authentication and account management | Yes |
| **Name** | User identification and display | Yes |
| **User ID** | Session management and data association | Yes |

### Audio Data

| Data Type | Purpose | Linked to Identity |
|-----------|---------|-------------------|
| **Voice Recordings** | Voice command processing and AI assistant | No |
| **Transcribed Text** | AI assistant queries | No |

Voice data is processed by OpenAI's Whisper service for speech-to-text conversion. Audio is not permanently stored and is not linked to your identity.

### Business Data

As an admin application, you will access business data including:
- Customer orders and information
- Product inventory data
- Vendor and partner information
- Business analytics and reports

This data belongs to 365Weapons and is accessed only by authorized administrators.

We use the collected information for:

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- API keys and credentials are stored securely in the iOS Keychain
- No sensitive data is stored in plain text on the device

### Network Security
- All network communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
- No unencrypted data transmission

### Access Control
- The App is restricted to authorized administrators only
- Authentication is required to access any functionality

## Data Retention
